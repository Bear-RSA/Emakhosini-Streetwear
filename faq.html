FAQ

Where do you ship?

We currently offer complimentary door-to-door shipping within South Africa on orders over R5,000. International shipping is available and calculated at checkout based on your location.

How do I track my order?

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email containing your tracking number and a link to track the delivery status of your package.

What is your sizing like?

Our garments are designed with a modern, slightly oversized fit to reflect contemporary streetwear aesthetics. We recommend taking your usual size for the intended fit, or sizing down if you prefer a more tailored look. Please refer to our Size Guide on each product page for detailed measurements.

Are your items restocked?

Many of our pieces are produced in limited quantities to maintain exclusivity and ensure the highest quality. While core essentials may be restocked, seasonal items and limited edition drops often will not return once sold out.
